Welcome to Dimple

Established in 1991, we are specialists in the field non-woven fabrics. Our product lines, used by professionals around the world, offer the best possible results in industrial applications.

Please take this opportunity to explore our new website, offering further information on our history, the business solutions available to you, and detailed specifications on all the product lines.

Meet Our Leadership

Ge Limmen

Chief Executive Officer

With over three decades of experience in the non-woven fabric industry, Ge Limmen founded Dimple in 1991 and continues to lead the company as CEO. Under his visionary leadership, Dimple has grown from a local startup to one of the market leaders in industrial non-woven fabrics, serving professionals across Europe and beyond.

Ge's commitment to innovation and excellence has driven the development of cutting-edge solutions, including the revolutionary High Speed Roller (HSR) 1700, which has transformed production efficiency in graphic printing applications.

It will be our pleasure to support you. Since starting Dimple, the industry has seen a number of changes. We strive to offer our customers cutting edge technology and business solutions. To this day and looking forward, our strong relationships both locally and worldwide allow us to develop the means share the ways to success.

- Ge Limmen, November 2014

Our Story & Expertise Three Decades of Excellence

Our History

Founded in 1991 by Ge Limmen in Uitgeest, The Netherlands. Over three decades of experience has made us market leaders in non-woven fabrics, delivering quality products and independent advice to professionals worldwide.

Market Leadership

Specialists in graphical, packaging, and display industries. Our 'never out of stock' principle ensures extremely short lead times and reliable supply chains for all your non-woven fabric needs.

Innovation Focus

At the forefront of technology development, including our revolutionary High Speed Roller (HSR) 1700. We create cutting-edge solutions that transform production efficiency.

Custom Solutions

Don't see what you need? We develop custom materials for specific applications. Our experience in creating today's standard products means we can design the perfect solution for your business.

Trusted By Our Machines Industry Leaders Worldwide

Our products are used on heatset, coldset, sheetfed and digital printers.
