Wash Cloth Industry-Leading Wiping Technology

Over the years, Dimple BV has grown into the role of specialists in the field of washcloth for the international Print Industry. In offset, we offer excellent cleaning cloths for automatic blanket cleaning systems. Additionally, we have the ideal material for automatic impression cylinder cleaning. For web presses, Dimple has developed a product for the systems, using wet material.

Premium Our Wash Cloth Solutions Industry-Leading Technology

Comprehensive range of specialized washcloths for every printing application, from offset to direct image printing and hand wiping solutions.

Offset Printing

Three premium washcloths for Automatic Blanket Cleaning systems: HeavyDuty®, Cum Laude®, and MultiSorb®. Available in ready-made mini rolls and 500-metre master rolls up to 2.50 metres wide. Plus CylinderClean® for perfect impression cylinder cleaning without wear.

Direct Image Printing

Ultimo® cloth for optimal plate cleaning with minimal scratching. MultiClean® washcloth with ImpregBox® or ImpregWinder® for web-printing, offering excellent cleaning at competitive rates with flexible wash product options.

Hand Wipes

BioCleaner® pre-saturated wipes for Press, Degrease, Glass, and Hand applications. Dry wipes available in plain and perforated rolls. In-house converting ensures short lead-times and reliable delivery.

Production Excellence

State-of-the-art converting technology since Q4 2007, enabling high flexibility in production and optimal output. Custom solutions available for specific requirements.
