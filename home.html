HSR 1700 • Racing Against Time

The Challenge

It took Dimple three years to design the High Speed Roller 1700. The aim was to speed up the production process of the winding of washcloth for the graphic printing industry and increase the efficiency of this process.

The Victory

All expectations were met when the first HSR 1700 became operational in October 2013. A true testament to engineering excellence and innovation.
